Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD)

A mental health disorder which may involve persistent difficulties with attention, hyperactivity and/or impulsivity (Mayo Clinic, 2021).

BC Interior ADHD Clinic

1340 Ellis Street, Kelowna, BC V1Y 9N1

250-861-7301

bcinterioradhdclinic.com

The Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) clinic provides diagnostic assessment for children (up to 12 years), youth (13-17 years) as well as adults (18+ years) in the Okanagan area to rule ADHD in/out. The ADHD Clinic also provides education for clients and families as well as support for treatment and intervention.

No fee.

No Referral Required.

Oak Learning

#112-1890 Cooper Road, Kelowna, BC V1Y 8B7

250-549-6958

oaklearning.ca

Oak Learning is a cognitive development program aimed at strengthening cognitive weaknesses in individuals with ADHD as well as learning difficulties by retraining the brain specifically in areas related to attention such as working memory and concentration. All ages.

$75.00-$125.00 (per session).

No Referral Required.

Okanagan Ability Center

2040 Springfield Road, Kelowna, BC V1Y 9N7

All ages.

Anxiety / Stress Management

Anxiety may be intense, excessive, or persistent worry which interferes with daily functioning (Mayo Clinic, 2021).

Stress management provides tools to promote resilience following an overactive sympathetic nervous system (Mayo Clinic, 2021).

Bullying, Self-Harm and Suicide Prevention

Bullying is a form of aggression where one is repeatedly and purposefully targeted physically, verbally, psychologically, socially, or electronically (Mayo Clinic, 2021).

Self-harm is an act of intentional harm to one-self (Mayo Clinic, 2021).

Suicide prevention aims to identify clients experiencing overwhelming thoughts of not wanting to live (Mayo Clinic, 2021).

Dementia and Alzheimer's

Alzheimer’s is a brain disorder that causes a continuous decline in cognition that impacts behaviour, social skills, and independent functioning (Mayo Clinic, 2021).

Dementia includes symptoms that negatively impact memory, cognitive, and social skills which interfere with daily living (Mayo Clinic, 2021).
