Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD)

Clinique TDAH de Médipsy - Medipsy ADHD Clinic

4610 Saint-Catherine St, Westmount, Quebec H3Z 1S3

514-419-3005

www.cognitionclinic.ca

The diagnosis of ADHD in adults is done using a combination of clinical interviewing, standardized questionnaires, and cognitive testing. Current research indicates that a complete neuropsychological assessment is not necessary in all cases for the diagnosis of ADHD in adults.

Cost for the Standard Clinical Evaluation is $1050, Comprehensive Neuropsychological Assessment is $165.

No Referral Required.

Psychological services provided by a registered psychologist are covered by most extended health insurance plans. Inquire with your insurance company to confirm your coverage.

Substance Abuse and Addiction

Addiction Outreach Program

6900 Decarie Blvd, Côte Saint-Luc, Quebec, H3X 2T1

514-731-8000 or addictionoutreach@hotmail.com

www.addictionoutreach.ca/english

Addiction Outreach Program (AOP) is a non-profit organization which provides intensive outpatient therapeutic and practical services for individuals suffering from drug, alcohol, and gambling abuse. A wide range of services are provided by certified professional counsellors in order to meet the specific needs of each individual. Other more specialized services are also offered on request.

AOP is a private non-profit organization and the fees are calculated depending on the income of each applicant. Fees are calculated according to a sliding scale structure. Tax receipts are also issued.

No Referral Required.
